Cake Decorating

The first cake I remember decorating was one for my Mom; I think it was a butterfly. In college, while Dan and I were dating, I made him a Tigger cake. Then once I had kids, it was a tradition every year for me to decorate a cake according to their current interests. More recently, Josie has taken over the majority of the decorating, even for her own cake.
