Feeling Crafty

Creating things is fun. I like making things with and for my children. I specialize in trunk or treat decorations and crocheted hats, but throw in some random stuff when time allows. Every October I participate in the Cancer Crochet Challenge hosted by Sweet Potato 3 to make hats for cancer fighters and survivors.
